World's Best Banana Pancakes

Crowd pleasing banana pancakes made from scratch. A fun twist on ordinary pancakes.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed

Directions

  1. Add white sugar, flour, salt, and baking powder into a bowl. Then, combine and mix milk, an egg, vegetable oil, and bananas in another.
  2. Next, stir the flour mixture with the banana mixture.
  3. Then, heat a lightly oiled frying pan or griddle over medium high heat.
  4. Pour the batter onto the frying pan with 1/4 cup for each pancake.
  5. Finally, cook until the pancakes are golden brown on each side and serve.
Prep Time: 10 min, Cook Time: 10 min
